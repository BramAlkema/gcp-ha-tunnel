Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This privacy policy describes how your Home Assistant smart home integration ("the Service") handles your data when connected to Google Home.

Data Collection

The Service acts as a bridge between your self-hosted Home Assistant instance and Google Home. We collect and process:

  • Device states: Current status of your smart home devices (on/off, brightness, temperature, etc.)
  • Device commands: Instructions from Google Home to control your devices
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens to verify your identity

Data Storage

All device data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently. Your data remains on:

  • Your local Home Assistant instance (under your control)
  • Google's servers (subject to Google's privacy policy)

This service only acts as a tunnel and does not retain any personal data or device information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transmitted between:

  • Your Home Assistant instance
  • Google Home services

Security

All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS. Authentication is handled via OAuth 2.0.

Your Rights

You can disconnect the Google Home integration at any time through the Google Home app, which will revoke access to your devices.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Home Assistant instance administrator.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.
